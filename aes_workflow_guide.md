# AES Engineering Project Workflow Guide

## Overview
This comprehensive workflow guide provides AES design staff with a step-by-step process for managing projects from initiation to completion, integrated with our PROCESS methodology and PEP (Project Execution Plan).

## How to Use This Workflow

### 1. **Project Start**
- Begin with the PEP document (`aes_pep.html`)
- Fill out Section 1: "P - Plan with Precision"
- Use the workflow chart to guide each phase

### 2. **PROCESS Integration**
The workflow is built around our PROCESS methodology:
- **P** - Plan with Precision
- **R** - Review and Reinforce  
- **O** - Own and Optimize
- **C** - Communicate
- **E** - Execute
- **S1** - Serve with Standards
- **S2** - Sustain and Scale

### 3. **Key Milestones & Checkpoints**

#### **30% Design Review**
- Complete concept development
- Verify code compliance
- Coordinate with client
- Update PEP Section 2: "R - Review & Reinforce"
- Check QA items 1-4 in PEP

#### **60% Design Review**
- Finalize detailed design
- Complete system calculations
- Select equipment
- Coordinate all disciplines
- Update PEP Section 4: "O - Own & Optimize"
- Check QA items 5-8 in PEP

#### **90% Design Review**
- Complete construction documents
- Finalize specifications
- Verify costs
- Update PEP Section 5: "C - Communicate & E - Execute"
- Check QA items 9-12 in PEP

### 4. **Issue Resolution Process**

When issues arise (red nodes in flowchart):

#### **Level 1: Standard Resolution**
- Document the issue in PEP communication log
- Implement quick fix
- Update documentation
- Continue with normal flow

#### **Level 2: Technical Issues**
- Escalate to senior engineer
- Conduct engineering review
- Verify code compliance
- Document resolution

#### **Level 3: Coordination Issues**
- Schedule multi-discipline meeting
- Resolve conflicts
- Adjust timeline if needed
- Update all stakeholders

#### **Level 4: Client Issues**
- Schedule client meeting
- Clarify requirements
- Manage scope changes
- Assess budget impact

#### **Level 5: Critical Issues (Purple Escalation)**
- Immediate PM notification
- Senior management involvement
- Strategic decision making
- Client relationship management

### 5. **Regular Checkpoints**

#### **Weekly Checkpoints** (Orange nodes)
- Review progress against PEP milestones
- Identify potential issues early
- Check resource allocation
- Update stakeholder communication

#### **Bi-weekly Checkpoints**
- Client communication review
- Quality assessment
- Timeline verification
- Process improvement identification

#### **Monthly Checkpoints** (Construction Phase)
- Construction progress review
- Issue tracking and resolution
- Client updates
- Quality assurance

### 6. **Quality Assurance Integration**

#### **QA Checkpoints** (Blue nodes)
- **QA1 (30% Review)**: Technical and client review
- **QA2 (60% Review)**: Multi-discipline and specification review  
- **QA3 (90% Review)**: Final review and client approval

### 7. **PEP Integration Points**

#### **Update PEP at Each Milestone:**
- **Planning Phase**: Sections 1 & 2
- **Design Phase**: Sections 3, 4 & 5
- **Construction Phase**: Section 5 & 6
- **Closeout Phase**: Section 6

#### **Track KPIs Throughout:**
- On-time deliverables
- Timesheet compliance
- Zero rework index
- Client satisfaction
- AES standards compliance
- Process improvement adoption
- Knowledge capture
- Team satisfaction

### 8. **Best Practices**

#### **Communication**
- Document all decisions in PEP communication log
- Follow client communication cadence
- Use change management process
- Maintain stakeholder engagement

#### **Quality**
- Use AES standards and templates
- Conduct peer reviews at each milestone
- Verify code compliance
- Coordinate across disciplines

#### **Efficiency**
- Track actual vs. estimated hours
- Identify process improvements
- Capture lessons learned
- Reuse successful strategies

#### **Sustainability**
- Update templates and tools
- Share knowledge with team
- Improve processes for future projects
- Maintain client relationships

### 9. **Emergency Procedures**

#### **Critical Issues**
1. Stop work immediately
2. Notify PM and senior staff
3. Assess impact and options
4. Communicate with client
5. Implement resolution
6. Update project plan
7. Continue with quality assurance

#### **Client Complaints**
1. Listen and document concerns
2. Acknowledge and apologize if appropriate
3. Investigate root cause
4. Develop resolution plan
5. Implement solution
6. Follow up to ensure satisfaction
7. Capture lessons learned

### 10. **Success Metrics**

Track these metrics in PEP Section 6:
- **Project Delivery**: On time, on budget, quality standards
- **Client Satisfaction**: Regular feedback, repeat business
- **Process Improvement**: Efficiency gains, reduced rework
- **Team Development**: Skill building, job satisfaction
- **Knowledge Management**: Lessons captured, templates updated

## Integration with PEP Document
